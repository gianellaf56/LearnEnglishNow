What is L.E.N?

As our melting pot expands each and every day, many newcomers experience difficulty learning English. And with busy schedules, it may be hard to schedule time to meet with a tutor or class. We created the game LEN(Learn English Now). This app helps newcomers learn English at their own pace, and is free of cost. The app allows for easy access at any time of the day and provides supplementary quizzes and videos. After a supplementary diagnostic, we evaluate your background in english and provide a set of game questions, personalized.
